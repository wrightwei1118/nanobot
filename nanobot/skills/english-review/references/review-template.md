## Report Guidelines

- Keep the tone encouraging but honest — highlight real progress without inflating it.
- Always compare against the previous week to show trajectory.
- The "Next Week Plan" should directly inform `english-train`'s topic selection.
- Archive score rows older than 14 days from MEMORY.md into HISTORY.md during review.
